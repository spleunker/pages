About me:

As you already may know my name is Neo, and welcome, to my website! I’m a GNU/Linux user, a trekkie and an avid lover of history. I’m also the president of a D&D(Dungeons & Dragons) club at my school; by the way, if you’d like to see any future, homemade D&D campaigns made by yours truly, check out my FTP server!

Anyway, feel free to explore ‘round the site-as of yet, I know there isn’t much as I’ve only recently finished setting up the theme of this site and what I was using to host it in the first place. So if you’ve been to this site a few times you’ll see tons of changes have been made. Most obvious being, the theme, which I’ve decided to make look more like the plan9 operating system from Bell Labs. I’ve also decided to generate this static site using Hugo a static site generator; the theme I’m using is a modification of Eric Murphy’s starter theme which I’ve used as the sort of “base” for the theme of this site, which I would totally recommend if you’re interested in making your own Hugo theme and in general just learning about how they work. Introductions to myself and the website aside, I hope you enjoy your stay here!
